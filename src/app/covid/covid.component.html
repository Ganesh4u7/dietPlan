Fight Covid-19

How to fight COVID-19 ?

Since there is no available vaccine yet , what we can do is to make our body ready to fight this virus if we are infected. How ? by strengthening our immune system.

So, What is Immune System ?
  • Immune system is the body’s multi-level defence network against potentially harmful bacteria, viruses and other organisms.
  • Our immune system is capable of developing antibodies to fight off bugs, as well as capacity to remember what the best methods were to attack previous invaders so that a future exposure will be much less problematic.This is how immunity is built up and the basis of how vaccines work.

Until a vaccine is available, our immune systems will need to adapt unaided to COVID-19.

There are no scientifically proven direct links between lifestyle and enhanced immune function.But that doesn't mean the effects of lifestyle on the immune system aren't intriguing and shouldn't be studied. General healthy-living strategies are a good way to start giving your immune system the upper hand.

How to strengthen your Immune System ?
  • No Smoking
  • Drinking little or no alcohol
  • Sleeping well
  • Eating a balanced diet
  • Taking regular moderate exercise
  • Maintaining a Healthy Weight ( You can know about this in our Body Analysis Page )
  • Importantly reducing stress

VITAMIN D

It’s best to get most of your vitamins from food, but vitamin D may be the exception to that rule. You can increase your intake through foods such as fatty fish (salmon, tuna and sardines) and fortified foods such as milk, orange juice and cereals.

Many people have a hard time absorbing vitamin D from food, so if you have a vitamin D deficiency, talk to your doctor about supplements.

The sun is our best natural source of vitamin D. Spending even a short time in the sun can provide the body with all of the vitamin D it needs for the day. To maintain healthy blood levels, aim to get 10–30 minutes of midday sunlight ,but Your exposure time should depend on how sensitive your skin is to sunlight
